About SlumSense

We're on a mission to transform urban informal settlements through data-driven insights, predictive analytics, and community-centered solutions.

Our Mission & Vision

SlumSense was founded with a singular vision: to leverage technology and data analytics to improve living conditions in urban informal settlements across India.

We believe that data-driven insights can empower governments, NGOs, and communities to make informed decisions, allocate resources effectively, and create sustainable change in some of the most vulnerable urban areas.

Our platform combines real-time monitoring, predictive analytics, and interactive visualization tools to provide comprehensive solutions for urban development challenges.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive analytical and predictive solutions to address urban settlement challenges

Predictive Analytics

  • Water Availability Forecasting

    Predict water availability in different zones based on historical data, weather patterns, and consumption trends

  • Garbage Overflow Rate Prediction

    Forecast garbage accumulation rates to optimize waste collection schedules and resource allocation

  • High-Risk Zone Identification

    Identify areas with high risk of sanitation issues, water shortages, or health hazards based on specific dates and conditions

Analytical Insights

  • Water Stress Index Analysis

    Calculate and visualize water stress levels across different regions to identify critical areas needing intervention

  • Complaints Resolution Tracking

    Monitor total vs. resolved complaints to measure service efficiency and identify improvement areas

  • Resource Gap Analysis

    Compare water availability vs. requirement and garbage collection capacity vs. generation rates
